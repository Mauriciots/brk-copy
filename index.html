A Vancouver favorite

Breka Bakery & Café is a local family business born in 2006 in the lively Vancouver neighbourhood of Sunset. Regularly voted among Vancouver’s best bakeries and cafes, it offers high-quality and reasonably priced coffee, breads, donuts, pastries, sandwiches, cakes, cookies and more – 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year – in each of its 6 locations on Fraser Street, Bute Street, Davie Street, West 4th Avenue, Denman Street and Main Street.
